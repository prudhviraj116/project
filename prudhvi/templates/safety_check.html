Safety Checks

Why Safety Checks are Important

Safety checks are essential to ensure that your vehicle is in proper working condition and safe to drive. Regular safety checks can help identify potential issues before they become major problems, reducing the risk of accidents and breakdowns.

Essential Safety Checks

To keep your vehicle in top condition, regularly perform the following safety checks:

  • Brakes: Ensure that your brakes are functioning properly and check for any signs of wear or malfunction.
  • Tires: Inspect tire pressure and tread depth. Look for any signs of damage or irregular wear.
  • Lights: Verify that all headlights, taillights, and indicators are working correctly.
  • Fluid Levels: Check engine oil, coolant, brake fluid, and windshield washer fluid levels.
  • Battery: Examine the battery for any signs of corrosion and ensure it is securely mounted.
