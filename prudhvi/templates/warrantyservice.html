Warranty and Service Plans

Warranty Information

Our comprehensive warranty coverage ensures peace of mind for your Royal Enfield motorcycle. Here’s what’s included:

  • Duration of Warranty: 2 years or 24,000 miles, whichever comes first
  • Coverage Details: Covers parts and labor for manufacturer defects, excluding wear-and-tear items
  • Claim Process: Contact your nearest authorized Royal Enfield dealer to file a warranty claim. Please bring your warranty booklet and proof of purchase.

Service Plans

Our service plans are designed to keep your motorcycle running smoothly and efficiently. Choose the plan that best fits your needs:

  • Regular Maintenance Plan: Includes oil changes, fluid checks, and general inspections every 5,000 miles.
  • Extended Service Plan: Covers all scheduled maintenance for 3 years, including priority scheduling and free roadside assistance.
  • Benefits: Enjoy benefits such as discounted parts and labor, priority service appointments, and annual vehicle health checks.
